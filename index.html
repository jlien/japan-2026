注 意 · Practical Notes

Things to remember

一Rainy season

Mid-June through mid-July is tsuyu across most of Honshu. Expect humid, sticky days with rain showers — pack a light rain jacket and have indoor backups for outdoor plans.

二Forward your bags

Yamato (takkyubin) ships luggage hotel to hotel for ¥2–3,000 a bag. Use it on every transition day: Tokyo → Fuji → Osaka → Toki → Haneda. Travel only with overnight bags.

三Suica / Pasmo

Get IC cards for everyone on arrival at Haneda. Welcome Suica is the tourist version. Tap on/off all trains, buses, and most convenience stores.

四Heat in late June

Osaka and Toki get hot and humid (28–32°C). Plan outdoor stops for morning and evening; reserve midday for arcades, food halls, museums, USJ shaded areas.

五Reservations for 5

A party of 5 is hard to seat at small Tokyo restaurants. Reserve via Tabelog or Pocket Concierge, or be ready to split into 2 + 3 at the counter.

六JR Pass math

Run your route through japan-guide.com's calculator before buying. With this itinerary, the 14-day pass may or may not pencil out — point-to-point tickets are often cheaper now.
